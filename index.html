Crystal Collector!

  • You will be given a random number from the start of the game.
  • There are four different colored crystals below. By clicking a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by successfully added up your total score to the given random number.
  • The value of the crystal is hidden until you click on it.
  • Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
  • Good luck!
